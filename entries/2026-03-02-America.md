The idea of America is beautiful. Life, Liberty, and the Pursuit of Happiness. These are the words the brave men who founded our country found to be perfect for their young nation. No matter how flawed, how broken, how fragile this country is, we still posses one of the most powerful and beautiful things. The American Spirit. The promise of this spirit has killed many dreamers, many who saw the American Dream. This promise is beautiful, yet broken. This “Dream” has changed form, time and time again, due to how unachievable it is for the common folk. “That dream of a land in which life should be better and richer and fuller for everyone”. This is no longer true, nor do i believe it ever has been. People are discriminated against everyday. People are unjustly removed from this country everyday for simply being a different skin color than everyone else. This is not a land where life is better and richer for everyone, it is a land that favors the few. Yet there are many people who hold the spirit of America near and dear to their hearts, and these people are the patriots.
